What's the big deal anyway?
Google Typhoid Mary. She was the first known carrier of Typhoid Fever and she prepared food for wealthy families. Typhoid Fever is spread via the fecal to oral route of contamination. In English, she pooped, didn't wash her hands, and handled foods like fruits and salads that are served without bringing their temperatures up. This day and age, Typhoid Fever is gone, but one way to get foodborne illness is poor hand washing.
